Cozy kitchen cinnamon toast

New Paragraph

  • 1 slice sprouted whole grain bread
  • coconut oil
  • a light drizzle agave
  • cinnamon

Toast the bread as light or dark as you like it. While it's still warm, spread a thin layer of coconut oil over the surface, along with a drizzle of agave. Lightly sprinkle cinnamon on top.
